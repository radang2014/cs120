Our Story

The four of us first met at the UCCMA Conference in 2017 at Simmons College in Boston, where we bonded over our love for American music and decided to form the Stars & Stripes Ensemble on the spot. With our combined musical talent, we have played gigs across the country, spreading our message of unity and pride through our performances.

Emily

With a passion for singing deeply rooted in her upbringing in Topeka, Kansas, Emily Thompson brings her powerful voice and dynamic stage presence as the lead vocalist. Trained in choral music with a degree from the Berklee College of Music and fueled by a dedication to spreading positivity and unity through her performances, Emily captivates audiences with soulful renditions of patriotic anthems and timeless classics, embodying the spirit of American music with every note she sings.

Mike

Michael Johnson brings dynamic energy and exceptional skill to his role as the lead percussionist. With a background steeped in jazz, rock, and classical percussion from his studies at Julliard, Mike's passion for rhythm and dedication to excellence drive the band's performances to new heights. Inspired by the diverse rhythms of American music, Mike's versatile playing style adds depth and intensity to the ensemble's patriotic repertoire.

Ben

Benjamin Rodriguez brings soulful melodies and vibrant energy as the band's skilled guitarist. Inspired by American folk and rock traditions from his upbringing in Cleveland, Ben's distinctive playing style blends blues, country, and rock influences, captivating audiences with fluid fretwork and emotive solos. With a humble demeanor and unwavering dedication to his craft, Ben embodies the spirit of musical innovation and patriotism.

Chris

Christopher Smith brings soulful melodies and unmatched passion to the Stars & Stripes Ensemble as the band's talented saxophonist. With roots in American jazz and blues from his studies at the New England Conservatory of Music, Chris's expressive playing style captivates audiences with smooth phrasing, dynamic improvisation, and heartfelt melodies. Celebrated for his unwavering dedication to his craft, Christopher embodies the essence of musical excellence and patriotism.
