With a passion for singing deeply rooted in her upbringing in Topeka, Kansas, Emily Thompson brings her powerful voice and dynamic stage presence to the Stars & Stripes Ensemble as the lead vocalist. Trained in choral music with a degree from the Berklee College of Music and fueled by a dedication to spreading positivity and unity through her performances, Emily captivates audiences with soulful renditions of patriotic anthems and timeless classics, embodying the spirit of American music with every note she sings.

Michael Johnson brings dynamic energy and exceptional skill to his role as the percussionist of the Stars & Stripes Ensemble. With a background steeped in jazz, rock, and classical percussion from his studies at Julliard, Michael's passion for rhythm and dedication to excellence drive the band's performances to new heights. Inspired by the diverse rhythms of American music, Michael's versatile playing style adds depth and intensity to the ensemble's patriotic repertoire, making him an indispensable member of the band and a true embodiment of musical excellence and patriotism.
