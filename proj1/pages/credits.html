Credits

This website was created as a project for the course CS 120: Web Programming & Engineering at Tufts University. The Stars & Stripes Ensemble is a band name that was made up for this project. Likewise, the names of the band members were also made up. Please do not actually show up to Boston Common on February 24th expecting a concert or try to contact this fictional band using the contact information listed. You will surely be disappointed if you do.

All acclaimed photos of band members are taken from photos posted on https://www.unsplash.com.

Acknowledgements

  • Professor Lisa Diorio for teaching many HTML and CSS tricks used on this website.
  • The internet at large, including sites like w3schools.com and stackoverflow.com for teaching me additional tricks, including the display: flex configuration in CSS.
  • ChatGPT 3.5 for helping write some of the content on this site, including the idea of naming the band the Stars & Stripes Ensemble, naming the band members, and generating first drafts of the introductory statement at the top of the homepage and bios for the band members (which I made minimal edits to).
