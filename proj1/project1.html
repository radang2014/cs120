Welcome to the home of the Stars & Stripes Ensemble, where we proudly dedicate our music to honoring the spirit of patriotism and the values that define our great nation. With a passion for performing heartfelt melodies that resonate with the American spirit, our four-person band based on Boston, MA is committed to delivering stirring performances that celebrate our heritage, unity, and freedom. Explore our website to discover more about our story, upcoming performances, and how we strive to inspire pride and patriotism through the power of music.

We are:

  • Emily Thompson, Vocals (left)
  • Michael Johnson, Percussion (middle left)
  • Benjamin Rodriguez, Guitar (middle right)
  • Christopher Smith, Saxophone (right)
